About Me

Nikolaos Boumparis holds a Psychology degree from the University of Crete and a Research Master's in Clinical and Developmental Psychopathology from Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. His Ph.D. at Vrije Universiteit focused on digital mental health interventions. Since 2021, Nikolaos has been a postdoctoral researcher at the Swiss Research Institute for Public Health and Addiction, conducting randomized controlled trials, systematic reviews, and meta-analyses in addiction research.

Research Focus:

  • Digital interventions focused on behavioral change
  • Addiction
  • Randomized controlled trials
  • Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
