About Me

Dr. Nikolaos Boumparis is a highly qualified clinical psychologist with a PhD in the field. With extensive experience in both research and clinical practice, Dr. Boumparis specializes in digital health interventions and their applications in psychology.

His research interests include:

  • E-health and m-health applications in mental health
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) in digital formats
  • The effectiveness of online psychological interventions

Dr. Boumparis is committed to advancing the field of psychology through innovative research and practical applications of digital technologies in mental health care.
